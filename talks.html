《铸视未来:大数据及人工智能系列报告会》 (Visual Forecast Talks)

《铸视未来:大数据及人工智能系列报告会》是同济大学智能大数据可视化实验室(Intelligent Big Data Visualization Lab, 即 iDVx Lab)主办的系列讲座活动。该活动旨在让大家了解大数据可视化领域的研究前沿，发现大数据科学的魅力。自2017年以来，实验室已成功主办了20余场，邀请了大数据领域内海内外知名专家、学者、研究人员等作为嘉宾进行演讲，主题包括了设计、可视化、数据挖掘、深度学习等领域内前沿趋势，吸引了许多学生及校外人士，广受大家的好评。

Visual Forecast Lectures is a series of talks organized by Intelligent Big Data Visualization Lab, Tongji University. It is designed to familiarize students with the research frontiers of data visualization and discover the power of data science. Since 2017, our lab has successfully hosted more than 20 talks, and invited famous experts, scholars, and researchers as visiting speakers. The topics include states of the arts in the field of design, visualization, data mining, and deep learning.
